Mapper 2.0

Immersal Mapper 2.0 is a powerful mapping tool designed for creating spatial maps. This documentation covers how to use Mapper 2.0 effectively.

Getting Started with Immersal Mapper 2.0

Immersal Mapper is a mapping tool for creating spatial maps in Immersal Cloud. The app is designed to capture the spatial features of a scene from different viewpoints and under different lighting conditions. Capturing the same scene multiple times from different angles increases the robustness of the map in terms of AR relocalization. Maps created using the app can be used to anchor digital objects, create navigation paths, or other location-based AR features.

Requirements

  • iPhone XS/XR or newer iOS device
  • An Internet connection (Wi-Fi recommended for uploading)
  • Immersal Developer Account

Mapping Best Practices

To create the best maps for localization, follow these practices:

  • Scan in good lighting conditions
  • Move slowly and steadily while scanning
  • Capture the environment from different angles
  • Include distinctive visual features in your maps
  • Avoid highly reflective or transparent surfaces

Using the Mapper App

The Mapper app interface consists of several key components:

Capturing Images

Point your device at the area you want to map and tap the capture button. Move around the space to capture images from different viewpoints.

Processing Maps

After capturing images, they are processed in the Immersal Cloud to create your spatial map.

Managing Maps

You can view, edit, and delete your maps through the Mapper app or the Immersal Developer Portal.

Note: For best results, ensure your device has sufficient storage and battery life before starting a mapping session.

Advanced Features

Mapper 2.0 includes several advanced features for professional applications:

  • Multi-session mapping
  • Map alignment tools
  • Sparse and dense point cloud visualization
  • GPS alignment for outdoor mapping
